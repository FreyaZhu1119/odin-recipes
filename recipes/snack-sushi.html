Steven's Snack Sushi

Sushi so good that you won't miss fish!

Snack Sushi from Steven Universe is perfect to make to share with a friends (or you can eat it all in one if you are a large pink lion!)

"This is a great dish for you to make if you're going to have friends over who don't like the taste of raw fish!" - Steven Universe

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • Mayonnaise
  • Your favorite hot sauce (we recommend Sriracha)
  • Sushi rice
  • ½ cup of rice vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 Sheets of nori
  • 1 Bag smooth cheese puffs

Directions

Make Sushi Rice:
  1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, rice vinegar, vegetable oil, and salt on medium heat. Cook until the sugar dissolves. Combine with the rice.
  2. Mix the rice until there is no more liquid and it is cool to touch.
Assembling the Sushi:
  1. Slice the avocado in half. Using your knife, extract the pit. Make vertical cuts on the inside of the avocado making sure not to pierce the skin. Use a spoon to scoop out the cut avocados. Set them aside.
  2. Put the seaweed paper on top of the sushi roller. Wet your fingers so the rice doesn’t stick to your hands. Add a generous amount of sushi rice to the paper. Wet your fingers again and press down the sushi rice.
  3. Add the sliced avocados in a line in the middle of the sushi rice. Add the cheese puffs next to the avocados.
  4. Roll the sushi using the bamboo roller making sure to press down, forming the sushi shape. Cut the sushi into pieces.
  5. Combine the mayonnaise with the hot sauce. Add a dollop onto each sushi piece.
