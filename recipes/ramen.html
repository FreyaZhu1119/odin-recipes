Ponyo Ramen 🍜

Hot ramen topped with some yummy ham and gooey egg - perfect for a rainy night!

The ramen that S≈çsuke's mom makes for Ponyo is so good that you might believe it's magical!

"IT'S HAAAAAAAAAM!!!!" - Ponyo
Ingredients
  • One package Nissan Chicken Ramen or your favorite instant ramen
  • 1-2 Slices of ham
  • Eggs
  • Green onions
Directions
  1. Add 1 inch of water to a sauce pot, cover, and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Once boiling, add an egg (or however many you'd like as long as they are in a single layer in the bottom of the pot), straight from the refrigerator into the pot. Replace the lid and let it continue to boil for exactly six minutes.
  3. After six minutes, remove the egg(s) from the pot and place them in an ice water bath or run under cool water until they are cool enough to handle. Peel and cut in half.
  4. Cut your green onions and slice your ham as thick or as thin as you'd like it.
  5. Follow the instructions on the back of your ramen package to cook the ramen.
  6. Transfer the ramen to a bowl and cover with a plate. Make your guest close their eyes and then perform the magic (assemble with half an egg, ham, and green onions)!
  7. Uncover and enjoy!

Recipe from Feast of Fiction but the hard boiled egg was changed to be soft boiled!
