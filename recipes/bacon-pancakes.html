Jake the Dog's Bacon Pancakes 🥓 🥞

Crispy bacon paired with some warm fluffy pancakes doused in maple syrup. It's a breakfast of champions!

The perfect breakfast from Adventure Time that will give you energy to save the princess in your life!

"Bacon pancakes, makin' bacon pancakes,

Take some bacon and I'll put it in a pancake,

Bacon pancakes, that's what it's gonna make,

Bacon pancaaake!" - Jake the Dog

Ingredients

  • Bacon strips
  • 1 1/2 Cups all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 Teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 Tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 Cups milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • Maple Syrup
Directions
Make Pancake Batter:
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  2. Sift twice
  3. Beat together egg and melted butter LIGHTLY until blended. Lightly beat in milk.
  4. Make a well in the center, pour wet mixture in.
  5. Mix until smooth - do NOT overmix
  6. Let sit for 10 minutes
Make Baked Bacon: (You can also just fry it in a pan)
  1. Line baking sheet with foil
  2. Put bacon slices on foil and into cold oven
  3. Heat oven to 400F
  4. 17-20 minutes, watch until bacon is golden brown. Depends on how fast your oven reaches 400.
  5. Take bacon out and immediately remove from baking pan. Blot with paper towels.
Make Bacon Pancakes:
  1. Heat lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour batter onto griddle.
  2. Wait until bubbles form, flip.
  3. Douse in maple syrup and enjoy!
